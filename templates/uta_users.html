Lecturer

Lecturers have different requirements for their assignments. They usually want to allocate their students into groups of various sizes. Sometimes they may even differentiate between their level of study and expertise. Forming teams, however, is both difficult and time consuming. Having all that in mind, UTA has been developed with the one and only purpose: to help lecturers form well balanced teams for their students as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Key Functions:

- Define the assignment requirements
- Upload list of students
- Define a limit for arranging teams
- Allocate the rest of the students through the system

Critical Needs:

- Form teams quickly and efficiently
- Monitor the process

Student

Students are usually enrolled into multiple courses requiring group assignments. Based on their past experience, however, they worry every time when it comes to forming groups. They either do not know enough colleagues so as to fulfill the requirements, or they were allocated with students who were not responsible at all, were not contributing and they were left doing the work on their own. The fact is that students get nervous when they have to form teams and work with other people. UTA is developed exactly to meet all students' needs and make the process of forming teams less painful and stressful.

Key Functions:

- Create a detailed qualifications profile
- Team up
- Accept/Decline suggested team

Critical Needs:

- To be allocated in a team
- To find a team in the time limit
- To find the best team

Opportunities to improve the user experience:

- Create your own team
- Get suggested team
- Meet on the go with apps
- Chat with team mates
- Upload files in the group page
- Notifications of upcoming events/deadlines
- Rate the team
